# Task-2-Data-Visualization-and-Story-telling

Sales Performance Dashboard – Business Insights

Sales Trend Over Time - Sales fluctuate across the year, helping identify seasonal demand and growth opportunities.
Sales by Category - Clothing generates the highest revenue and should remain a strategic focus.
Sales by Country - Portugal is the strongest-performing market.
Profit by Category - High sales do not always mean high profit.
Device type Distribution - Shows customer purchasing behavior across devices.
Sales by Sales Manager - Identify top-performing managers.

1. Overall Performance
   Present KPIs summarizing total sales, profit, and orders.
2. Sales Trend
   Show how revenue changes over time.
3. Best Products
   Clothing is the highest revenue category, followed by Games and Appliances.
4. Best Markets
   Portugal and France contribute the largest share of revenue.
5. Team Performance
   Compare sales managers and representatives to identify top performers.
6. Customer Behavior
   Device type analysis highlights how customers prefer to shop.
7. Key Recommendations
   Invest more in high-performing categories like Clothing.
   Strengthen marketing in top-performing countries (Portugal and France).
   Investigate underperforming categories to improve profitability.
   Reward top sales representatives and replicate their sales strategies.
